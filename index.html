What's On The Menu?

What's On The Menu is your solution to the "What do I cook tonight?" dilemma. We are here to help you in three different ways.

Step 1: Enter your preferred cuisine and any terms you would like to search by, you can leave either field blank if you wish.


Step 2: Select one of the three different search options below!

Recipe Search

This search will bring up a list of up to 10 popular recipes based on the criteria entered above.

Clean Out the Pantry

This search will provide you with up to 5 recipes based on the criteria entered above, that also use the ingredients specified below. The more ingredients entered, the more specialized the recipes will be (we recommend 3-5 ingredients). Great if you are looking to clean out some extra items in the kitchen.

Anything At All

This search will pull a completely random recipe up. For those who like to live dangerously.
