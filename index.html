What's On The Menu?

What's On The Menu is your solution to the "What do I cook tonight?" dilemma. We are here to help you in three different ways.

Simply select your preferred cuisine (or leave blank if you do not wish to specify a preference) and add any additional search terms you would like to filter by. Once you've done this, press one of the three search options below and enjoy! (If you want to Clean Out the Pantry, be sure to add your ingredients in the appropriate box as well before searching)

Recipe Search

This search will bring up a small list of popular recipes based on the criteria you entered above.

Clean Out the Pantry

This search will provide you with 5 recipes based on the criteria you entered above, that also use the ingredients you specify below, the more ingredients you enter, the more specialized the recipe will be (we recommend 3-5). Great if you are looking for a way to use up those tricky ingredients.

Anything At All

This search will pull a completely random recipe up for you. For those who like to live dangerously. If you entered a cuisine above, you will still get a recipe from that cuisine.
