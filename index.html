What's On The Menu?

What's On The Menu is your solution to the "What do I cook tonight?" dilemma. We are here to help you in three different ways.

  • The "Recipe Search" search will bring up a small list of popular recipes based on the criteria you enter.
  • The "Clean Out the Pantry" search will provide you with 5 recipes that use the ingredients you specify, great if you are looking for a way to use those tricky ingredients!
  • The "Anything At All" search will pull a completely random recipe up for you. Use this if you like to live dangerously. For those who like to live dangerously.

Simply select your preferred cuisine (or leave blank if you do not wish to specify a preference) and add any additional search terms you would like to filter by. Once you've done this, press one of the three search options below and enjoy! (If you want to Clean Out the Pantry, be sure to add your ingredients in the appropriate box as well before searching)
