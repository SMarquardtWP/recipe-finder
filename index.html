What's On The Menu?

What's On The Menu is your solution to the "What do I cook tonight?" dilemma. We are here to help you in three different ways.

  • The "Recipe Search" button will bring up a small list of popular recipes based on the criteria you enter.
  • The "Clean Out The Pantry" button will provide you with 5 recipes that use the ingredients you specify, great if you are looking for a way to use those tricky ingredients!
  • The "Anything At All" button will pull a completely random recipe up for you. Use this if you like to live dangerously. For those who like to live dangerously.
